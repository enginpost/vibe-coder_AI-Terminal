# Epic: AI Terminal V1 - AI Chat Management

**Product:** [AI Terminal](../../product-vision--ai-terminal.md)
**Version / Release Label:** V1 - AI Chat Management

## Goal Statement
Deliver the AI chat management capabilities of the AI Terminal app, enabling technical users to add, open, and manage one or more AI chat sessions within a single workspace, including manually copying highlighted text from the AI chat and pasting clipboard text into the AI Chat input.

## Success Criteria
*Each criterion must trace to a KPI or OKR defined in the product vision.*

- [Chat-to-Terminal Speed] — advances this metric by facilitating integrated AI chat that can directly copy highlighted chat text for transfer to the terminal via the clipboard.
